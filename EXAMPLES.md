## 1. Vocabulary Activity

**Main Intent:**
Learn food-related vocabulary for navigating a casual restaurant (A2 level, concrete domain).

**Example Cards:**

* waiter → der Kellner
* menu → die Speisekarte
* to order → bestellen
* bill → die Rechnung
* water (sparkling/still) → Wasser (mit/ohne Kohlensäure)
* tip → das Trinkgeld
* table for two → ein Tisch für zwei
* reservation → die Reservierung

---

## 2. Grammar Theory Activity

**Main Intent:**
Understand when to use dative vs accusative with common German prepositions.

**Example Cards:**

* Is “in dem Park” location or motion? → location
* Is “auf den Tisch” static or directional? → directional
* Which case does “mit” take? → always dative
* Which case does “für” take? → always accusative
* Sentence correction: Ich gehe mit den Park ❌ → in den Park ✔️

---

## 3. Grammar Processing Activity

**Main Intent:**
Train real-time interpretation of word order and case meaning in German sentences.

**Example Cards:**

* “Den Mann sieht der Hund” → The dog sees the man
* “Der Mann sieht den Hund” → The man sees the dog
* Contrast: “Ich gebe dem Mann das Buch” which is the direct object? / which is the indirect object?
* Modify: “Ich gebe dem Mann das Buch” to swap subject/recipient → “Der Mann gebe mir das Buch”
* Interpret: “Mit dem Freund gehe ich ins Kino”

---

## 4. Cultural Knowledge Activity

**Main Intent:**
Understand cultural norms around ordering and tipping in German-speaking countries.

**Example Cards:**

* Is tipping expected? → yes, small rounding or ~5–10%
* Do you ask for the bill or wait? → ask directly
* Phrase: “Zahlen bitte” → means “check please”
* Is water free? → usually no
* Typical greeting in restaurant → “Guten Tag”

---

## 5. Template Sentences (Procedure)

**Main Intent:**
Practice constructing ordering sentences using reusable templates.

**Example Cards:**

* “Ich hätte gern ___” → I would like ___
* “Könnte ich ___ bekommen?” → Could I get ___
* “Ich nehme ___” → I’ll take ___
* “Für mich ___ bitte” → For me ___ please
* Cue: Ask the waiter if a food contains an allergen

---

## 6. Grammar Manipulations

**Main Intent:**
Practice modifying sentences to include time, manner, or indirect objects.

**Example Cards:**

* Add time: “Ich esse” → “Ich esse heute Abend”
* Add indirect object: “Ich gebe das Buch” → “Ich gebe dem Mann das Buch”
* Change tense: “Ich gehe” → “Ich ging”
* Add modal: “Ich esse” → “Ich will essen”
* Negate: “Ich habe Hunger” → “Ich habe keinen Hunger”

---

## 7. Fundamentals (High-Repetition)

**Main Intent:**
Automate article + case transformations.

**Example Cards:**

* der Mann → dem Mann (dative)
* die Frau → der Frau (dative)
* das Kind → dem Kind (dative)
* ein Mann → einem Mann
* mein Freund → meinem Freund
* aus + der Laden → aus dem Laden

---

## 8. Reading Activity

**Main Intent:**
Read and understand a short restaurant interaction passage (~2 min reading).

**Example Cards (sentence-level SRS):**

* “Der Mann betritt das Restaurant.”
* “Er setzt sich an einen Tisch.”
* “Der Kellner bringt die Speisekarte.”
* “Er bestellt ein Wasser und ein Sandwich.”
* “Am Ende bezahlt er die Rechnung.”

---

## 9. Situation Activity (Dialogue)

**Main Intent:**
Practice ordering food at a restaurant through sub-situations.

**Example Cards (sub-situations):**

* entering and asking for a table
* ordering drinks
* asking questions about menu items
* ordering food
* asking for the bill
* clarifying a mistake in the order

---

## 10. Situation Activity (Writing)

**Main Intent:**
Write short responses in restaurant-related contexts.

**Example Cards:**

* Write how you would order a drink
* Ask where the bathroom is
* Complain politely about wrong food
* Ask for recommendations
* Write a short dialogue (2–3 lines)

---

## 11. Pronunciation Activity

**Main Intent:**
Improve pronunciation of common restaurant phrases using TTS/STT feedback.

**Example Cards:**

* “Ich hätte gern ein Wasser”
* “Die Rechnung bitte”
* “Haben Sie vegetarische Optionen?”
* “Ich nehme das Tagesgericht”
* “Danke schön”

---

## 12. Listening Activity

**Main Intent:**
Understand spoken restaurant phrases and respond.

**Example Cards:**

* (Audio) “Was möchten Sie trinken?” → What did they ask?
* (Audio) “Die Rechnung kommt sofort” → What will happen?
* (Audio) “Möchten Sie noch etwas?” → Respond appropriately
* (Audio) “Das macht 12 Euro” → What is the total?
* (Audio) “Wir schließen um 22 Uhr” → When do they close?

---

## 13. Errors SRS Activity

**Main Intent:**
Practice recurring personal mistakes collected from interaction sessions.

**Example Cards:**

* Error: “Ich will ein Wasser” → Better: “Ich hätte gern ein Wasser”
* Error: wrong case after “mit” → fix to dative
* Error: missing verb position in question
* Error: word order in subordinate clause
* Error: literal translation from English

---

## 14. Generic Conversation Activity

**Main Intent:**
Freely discuss food, preferences, and experiences while receiving feedback.

**Example Cards (conversation prompts):**

* Describe your favorite meal
* Talk about a restaurant experience
* Explain what you usually order
* Ask the tutor for recommendations
* Roleplay being a waiter
